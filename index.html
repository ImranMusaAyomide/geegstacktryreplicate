About Geegstack

Geegstack Academy is a software engineering school that teaches individuals with prior or zero coding experience the skill sets they need to become a hirable software engineers or develop their tech solution.

Why Geegstack?

Geegstack  was founded and managed by experienced techies who have not only worked at various tech niches, but also taught coding to people with diverse backgrounds. The academy ensure delivery for every student and this is achieved by keeping every student under the academy mentorship until they get a paid job or project or have the skillsets to develop their solution.
